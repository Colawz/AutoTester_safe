# Content Writing and Packaging

`content-writing-and-packaging` covers:

Tasks that convert source materials or gathered facts into polished writing, briefings, summaries, rewritten content, or curated packages.

## Coverage Note

These cases were favored when they started from source material or a concrete artifact contract, not when they were generic one-paragraph writing prompts.

## Current Families

- `content-humanization-rewrite`: Rewrite robotic source text into more natural prose while preserving meaning. Variants: reference.
- `daily-research-summary`: Read multiple internal research notes and turn them into an executive daily briefing. Variants: reference.
- `eli5-pdf-summary`: Translate a technical PDF into an accessible, audience-specific summary. Variants: reference.
- `email-triage-brief`: Review an inbox-like workspace and produce an action-oriented triage summary. Variants: reference.
- `recipe-cookbook-builder`: Collect recipe information and package it into a compact cookbook artifact. Variants: e1, m1, h1.
