Acme Corporation Annual Report 2025

Executive Summary
Acme Corporation achieved record revenue of $2.5 billion in 2025.
The company employs 12,000 people across 15 countries.
CEO Sarah Chen highlighted growth in the APAC region.

Financial Highlights
- Total Revenue: $2,500,000,000
- Net Income: $350,000,000
- R&D Spending: $450,000,000
- Operating Margin: 22%

Key Projects
1. Project Quantum - cloud infrastructure modernization
2. Project Nexus - AI-driven customer analytics platform
3. Project Horizon - sustainability initiative
