The early morning sun cast long shadows across the garden.
Birds began their daily chorus in the old oak tree.
A gentle breeze rustled through the cherry blossoms.
The ancient bronze sundial stood in the center of the garden.
Moss covered its surface, indicating its great age.
The gardener arrived promptly at six o'clock.
She inspected the rose bushes for any signs of disease.
The sundial was her favorite feature of the garden.
She often wondered who had placed it there centuries ago.
Today she planned to plant new lavender along the path.
